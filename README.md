This repository contains my completed exercises and projects for AMAT 131: Statistical Methods and Statistical Design and AMAT 132: Introduction to Forecasting. All work presented here is my original work, completed for learning and skill development purposes. While some exercises may be similar to those used in previous courses, all solutions are uniquely completed by me. Some parts, including the final projects for AMAT 132, were completed in collaboration with classmates.

Building and interpreting predictive models
Application of statistical methods to real-world forecasting scenarios
